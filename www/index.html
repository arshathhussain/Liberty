WELCOME TO LIBERTY LEATHER STORES


LIBERTY LEATHER STORES established in 1991, has quickly grown into a large professional supplier of metal fittings & other fashion accessories to shoes, Bags & Garments etc. “LIBERTY LEATHER STORES” caters to the input needs of the Domestic & Export Industry and has specialized in Eyelets, Buckles, rivet buttons and Ornaments.

TECHOLOGY OF EYELETS LIBERTY LEATHER STORE

There are Three Basic Types of Settings… Roll (or Coil), Scored, or Flared. The most common is the roll, Which provides a Smooth, Rounded, Uniform surface. A scored setting divides, The end of the Barrel into equal segments to provide extra grip Strength in soft materials. A Flared setting is Commonly used in PC Boards and Provides a appearance often used for soldering.
