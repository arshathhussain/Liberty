LIBERTY LEATHER STORES established in 1991, has quickly grown into a large professional supplier of metal fittings & other fashion accessories to shoes, Bags & Garments etc. “LIBERTY LEATHER STORES” caters to the input needs of the Domestic & Export Industry and has specialized in Eyelets, Buckles, rivet buttons and Ornaments.

Over 18 years' experience in Trading and Supply have led to a competitive and reliable service based industry. Our office is located in the INTERNATIONAL LEATHER MARKET AREA (PERIAMET) in Chennai City, South India in close proximity to all modes of transport facilities provides our products access to all the parts of the world.

LIBERTY LEATHER STORES is a young and vibrant organization, dedicated to the growth and development of the Indian Footwear Components supplier by lending impetus to the all round development of the Indian Footwear component industry. We have a formal relationship with the leading Export Houses in India, predominantly in south of India. Our products are tested for quality and “reliability on a regular basis by approving agencies like FDDI-Delhi / CLRI- Chennai / SGS-Chennai / TUV-Chennai.”
